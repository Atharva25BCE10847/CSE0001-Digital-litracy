# Social Media Do’s and Don’ts for College Students

## ✅ Do’s
- Think before posting anything online.
- Maintain a respectful and positive tone.
- Protect your privacy (limit personal information).
- Verify information before sharing (avoid fake news).
- Use social media for learning, networking, and growth.

## ❌ Don’ts
- Don’t post offensive, abusive, or inappropriate content.
- Don’t share fake or unverified information.
- Don’t overshare personal or sensitive details.
- Don’t engage in online arguments or trolling.
- Don’t ignore your digital footprint (future employers may see it).
